About Me
======
I am a Ph.D. student at Carnegie Mellon University's Language Technologies Institute, School of Computer Science, where I am advised by Dr. [Katerina Fragkiadaki](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~katef/) within the [Robotics, Embodied AI and Learning (REAL)](https://www.cmu.edu/real/research/index.html) Center.

My interests are: Multimodal AI / Embodied AI / Robot Learning

Before beginning my Ph.D., I worked as a research associate at the [AICV Lab](https://uark-aicv.github.io/) under Dr. [Ngan Le](https://uark-aicv.github.io/team/ngan_le) at the University of Arkansas and served as a visiting scholar with the [MLSP Group](https://cmu-mlsp.github.io/) led by Dr. [Bhiksha Raj](https://cmu-mlsp.github.io/team/bhiksha_raj) at Carnegie Mellon.
